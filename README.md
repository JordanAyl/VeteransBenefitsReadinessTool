Veteran Education & Financial Readiness Planner

A Streamlit application designed to help U.S. veterans plan their education pathway while modeling financial readiness using GI Bill benefits, savings, and projected expenses.

Built to bridge the gap between education planning and financial sustainability during the transition from military service to higher education.

Purpose

Veterans transitioning into college face two major challenges:

Understanding how long their GI Bill benefits will last

Determining whether their savings and monthly income can sustain them through graduation

This app integrates both into a single planning dashboard.

Example Use Case

A veteran starting college with:

24 months of GI Bill remaining

$60,000 in savings

$2,800 monthly BAH

$3,500 monthly expenses

The app projects:

When GI Bill benefits expire

Whether savings can sustain the remaining semesters

Total financial runway in months

Why I Built This

As a Navy veteran transitioning into Computer Science, I realized that most veterans manually estimate:

Benefit duration

Housing coverage

Graduation timelines

Savings burn rate

There was no single tool combining both academic and financial readiness.

This project demonstrates:

Practical problem solving

Financial modeling logic

Data visualization

User-focused application design
